Tribal Maps


This page showcases various maps of tribal distribution in Iranian Baluchestan.


‘FIG. 52. Tribal distribution.’ [‎256v] (2/2)

This is a general map of Iran's tribal distribution, c. 1945. In the southeast corner, we can see an approximate area of Baluch tribes, with some of them listed such as the Damani and Lashari.


Tribes of Southeastern Iran

This map shows tribes in southeastern Iran, in particular in the east of Makran and the Dezak/Saravan region. Prominent Baluch tribes like the Hot, Lashari, Damani, and non-Baluch tribes like the Baranzai, Dehwar, and Jadgal, are shown.
